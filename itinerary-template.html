3 Days Bwindi Gorilla Trekking

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, Uganda

Trip Overview

Experience the ultimate wildlife encounter in the ancient rainforests of Bwindi. This safari takes you deep into the jungle to spend an hour with the world's last remaining mountain gorillas.

Day 1

Arrival & Transfer to Bwindi

Pickup from Entebbe/Kampala. A scenic drive through the "Switzerland of Africa" with a stop at the Equator for photos. Arrive at your lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2

The Gorilla Trekking Experience

After an early breakfast and briefing, enter the forest with expert rangers. Once found, you will spend 1 hour observing these gentle giants in their natural habitat.

Day 3

Transfer back to Entebbe

Enjoy a final breakfast in the forest before heading back to Entebbe for your flight home, stopping for lunch in Mbarara.
