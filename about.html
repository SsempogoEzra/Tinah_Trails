Our Story

Guiding You Through the Pearl of Africa

Tinah Trails Safari was born out of a passion for Uganda's untouched wilderness. Based in Kampala, we specialize in showing travelers the hidden gems—from the misty peaks of the Rwenzori Mountains to the vast savannahs of Kidepo.

Our guides are local experts who understand the rhythms of the wild and the importance of conservation-led tourism.
